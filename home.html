Who We Are

OUR STORY OBJECTIVES DEPARTMENTS OUR TEAM

Ethical Manufacturing | Sustainable Sourcing | Quality Raw Material

DLM PLC Textile Manufacturing Has Been Established Since 1977 Ec In Conformity With Commercial Code Of Ethiopia As Private Limited Company and Put Up The Manufacturing Unit Startup Capital Of 1,618,705 Usd.

We Have Got Several Licenses For Manufacturing and Import For Various Plastic Raw Materials.

Company Has Been Located At Akaki Kality On Land Of 15000sq Meter ; We Have More Than 480 Local Workers and Few Expatriates At Present and As Per The Expansion Future Projects Will Be Targeted To 800 Local Workers.

Our Sister Company Is Known As Galaxy Industries PLC Indulged Into Plastics.
