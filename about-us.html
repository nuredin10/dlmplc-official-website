Who We Are

Our Story

DLM PLC Textile Manufacturing Has Been Established Since 1977 E.C (1985G.C) In Conformity With Commercial Code Of Ethiopia As Private Limited Company and Put Up The Manufacturing Unit Startup Capital Of 1,618,705 USD.

We Have Got Several Licenses For Manufacturing and Import For Various Plastic Raw Materials.

Company Has Been Located At Akaki Kality On Land Of 15000 sq. Meter; We Have More Than 480 Local Workers and Few Expatriates At Present and As Per The Expansion Future Projects Will Be Targeted To 800 Local Workers.

Our Sister Company Is Known As Galaxy Industries PLC Indulged Into Plastics.

Objectives

DLM prioritizes The Socio-Economic Well-Being of The Workers and Products Quality



At DLM, With Well-Defined Quality Objectives In Mind To Attain Customer Satisfaction, Is At The Heart Of Our Business Policy and Strategy. DLM Is Committed To Manufacturing, Source, Supply, Export and Trade Quality Textile Products And Services. We Will Always Endeavor To Satisfy Our Customers’ Needs and Exceed Expectations Through Win-Win Relationships and Delight Them. Our Vision Is To Serve Our Customers With Dedication and Offer Specialized Services With DLM Quality Assurance, Walking The ‘Extra Mile.’
